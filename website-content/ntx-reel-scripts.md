## REEL PRODUCTION TIPS

### **Equipment Needed:**
- Smartphone (iPhone/Android)
- Natural lighting (Texas sunshine!)
- Simple ring light (optional)
- CapCut or InShot app (free editing)

### **Filming Tips:**
1. **Shoot vertically** (9:16 ratio)
2. **Hook in first 1-2 seconds** (critical!)
3. **Use trending audio** (Instagram promotes these)
4. **Add captions** (80% watch without sound)
5. **Steady shots** (use tripod or prop phone)
6. **Good lighting** (natural light best)

### **Editing Tips:**
1. **Keep it snappy** (cut out pauses)
2. **Use transitions** (cut on beat)
3. **Text overlays** (easy to read, large font)
4. **CTA at end** (phone number, link in bio)
5. **Captions for accessibility**

### **Posting Strategy:**
- **Best times:** 6-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM (Texas time)
- **Frequency:** 3-5 per week
- **Consistency:** Same days each week
- **Engagement:** Reply to comments within 1 hour

### **Sound Strategy:**
- Check "Trending Audio" tab weekly
- Use sounds with 100K+ uses
- Mix trending sounds + original voiceover
- Save effective sounds for future use
