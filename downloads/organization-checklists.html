How to Use These Checklists

  • Print and Check: Print this page and physically check off completed items
  • Start Small: Don't try to do everything at once - pick one room or area
  • Set a Timer: Use the time estimates as guides, not strict rules
  • Get Help: Many tasks are easier with a friend or family member
  • Take Breaks: Rest when you need to - organizing is physical work!

üåÖ Daily Maintenance (15 minutes)

Estimated Time: 15 minutes | Frequency: Every Day

Morning Routine (5 minutes)

  • Make bed immediately upon rising
  • Put dirty clothes in hamper
  • Hang up tomorrow's outfit
  • Clear nightstand surfaces
  • Open blinds/curtains for natural light

Kitchen Reset (5 minutes)

  • Load/run dishwasher as needed
  • Wipe down counters after use
  • Put away items left on counters
  • Sort mail immediately
  • Take out trash when full

Evening Wind-Down (5 minutes)

  • Do a 5-minute pickup in main areas
  • Put items back in designated spots
  • Set up coffee maker for tomorrow
  • Check tomorrow's schedule
  • Turn off lights and electronics

üìÖ Weekly Organization (30 minutes)

Estimated Time: 30 minutes | Frequency: Once per week
  • Declutter one small problem area (junk drawer, countertop)
  • File accumulated paperwork
  • Clean out purse/wallet/backpack
  • Organize car interior and trunk
  • Review and update calendar for upcoming week
  • Do laundry and put away clean clothes
  • Plan meals for the upcoming week
  • Tidy up entryway and mudroom
  • Quick vacuum/sweep of main living areas
  • Empty all wastebaskets
  • Check supply levels (toilet paper, cleaning products)
  • Put away items that have accumulated in wrong rooms

üè† Room-by-Room Decluttering

Estimated Time: 2-4 hours per room | Frequency: 2-4 times per year

Kitchen Deep Organization

  • Clear and wipe down all counters
  • Go through pantry - check expiration dates
  • Organize spice rack/cabinet
  • Clean out refrigerator and freezer
  • Organize cabinets by function
  • Declutter gadgets and small appliances
  • Organize under-sink area
  • Sort through junk drawer
  • Check condition of dish towels and sponges
  • Wipe down inside of microwave and oven

Bedroom Reset

  • Strip and wash all bedding
  • Organize nightstand drawers
  • Clean out from under the bed
  • Go through dresser drawers
  • Organize jewelry and accessories
  • Check condition of pillows and mattress
  • Vacuum thoroughly including under bed
  • Dust all surfaces and lamps

Living Room/Family Room

  • Remove all items that don't belong
  • Organize entertainment center
  • Go through books and magazines
  • Check remote control batteries
  • Fluff and rotate couch cushions
  • Dust all surfaces including electronics
  • Vacuum furniture and floors
  • Organize throw blankets and pillows
  • Check and organize game/movie collection

Bathroom Deep Clean

  • Clear all surfaces completely
  • Go through medicine cabinet - check dates
  • Organize under-sink storage
  • Sort through cosmetics and toiletries
  • Check towel condition and replace if needed
  • Clean shower caddy or shelves
  • Organize cleaning supplies
  • Replace old or ratty shower curtain

üçÇ Seasonal Organization Checklist

Estimated Time: 3-5 hours | Frequency: 4 times per year

Montana Spring Transition (April-May)

  • Pack away heavy winter coats and gear
  • Bring out spring/summer clothing
  • Store winter boots; bring out hiking shoes
  • Switch out bedding to lighter weight
  • Organize camping gear for upcoming season
  • Check condition of outdoor furniture
  • Prepare garden tools and supplies
  • Clean and organize mudroom for less gear
  • Service and organize lawn care equipment

Montana Fall Preparation (September-October)

  • Bring out winter clothing and coats
  • Store summer-only items
  • Prepare mudroom for increased gear storage
  • Check heating system and supplies
  • Organize hunting gear (if applicable)
  • Switch to heavier bedding
  • Store garden tools and outdoor furniture
  • Stock up on winter emergency supplies
  • Organize firewood storage area

üì¶ Moving Preparation Checklist

Estimated Time: Multiple weeks | Start: 8 weeks before move

8 Weeks Before

  • Start decluttering - donate/sell unwanted items
  • Research moving companies and get quotes
  • Create moving binder/folder for documents
  • Start using up frozen and perishable foods
  • Take photos of valuable items for insurance

6 Weeks Before

  • Book moving company or truck rental
  • Start collecting moving supplies (boxes, tape, etc.)
  • Begin packing items you won't need immediately
  • Label boxes clearly with room and contents
  • Create inventory list of packed boxes

4 Weeks Before

  • Notify utilities of move date and new address
  • Update address with bank, insurance, employer
  • Arrange mail forwarding with post office
  • Schedule utility connections at new home
  • Arrange childcare/pet care for moving day

2 Weeks Before

  • Confirm moving company details and timing
  • Pack all non-essentials
  • Arrange cleaning services for old and new home
  • Back up computer files
  • Withdraw cash for moving day tips

1 Week Before

  • Pack everything except daily essentials
  • Prepare "first day" box with immediate needs
  • Confirm all arrangements (movers, utilities, etc.)
  • Pack cleaning supplies for both homes
  • Charge all electronic devices

Moving Day

  • Pack remaining essentials in clearly marked box
  • Do final walkthrough with camera/video
  • Keep important documents with you
  • Check inventory as items are loaded
  • Do final cleanup of old home

üéÑ Holiday Decoration Organization

Estimated Time: 2-3 hours | Frequency: After each major holiday

Post-Holiday Storage Checklist

  • Take photos of decorations in place for next year reference
  • Test all lights before storing - discard broken ones
  • Wrap delicate ornaments in tissue paper or bubble wrap
  • Store ornaments in divided boxes or egg cartons
  • Label boxes clearly with holiday and room location
  • Store garland and wreaths in large plastic bags
  • Keep instruction manuals with electronic decorations
  • Store all decorations in designated area
  • Make note of items to replace or add next year
  • Clean and vacuum area before storing boxes

Pre-Holiday Setup Checklist

  • Review photos from previous year for decoration ideas
  • Test all lights and electronics before decorating
  • Check condition of decorations - repair or replace as needed
  • Plan decoration placement room by room
  • Gather extension cords and timers
  • Clear surfaces before decorating
  • Store everyday items safely while decorations are up
