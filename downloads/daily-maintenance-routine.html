The 15-Minute Rule

Maintaining an organized home doesn't require hours of work. These simple daily habits take just 15 minutes but make the difference between chaos and calm. Consistency beats perfection!

üåÖ Morning Routine (5 minutes)

Start Your Day Right

  • Make your bed as soon as you get up
  • Put dirty clothes in hamper, not on floor
  • Clear nightstand surfaces
  • Open curtains/blinds to let in natural light
Montana Tip: During winter months, this morning light is especially important for mood and energy!

üç≥ Kitchen Habits (5 minutes)

Clean As You Go

  • Wash dishes immediately after eating
  • Wipe down counters after meal prep
  • Put items back in designated spots
  • Run dishwasher when full, empty when clean
  • Take out trash when full

üåô Evening Wind-Down (5 minutes)

Reset for Tomorrow

  • Do a 5-minute pickup in main living areas
  • Put items back where they belong
  • Lay out clothes for tomorrow
  • Prepare coffee maker or breakfast items
  • Clear dining table and kitchen island

üìã The One-Touch Rule

Handle Items Once

Instead of: Dropping mail on counter ‚Üí moving to table ‚Üí moving to desk ‚Üí finally dealing with it

Try this: Touch it once and put it where it belongs or deal with it immediately

Daily Applications:

  • Mail: Sort immediately (trash, file, action needed)
  • Dishes: Wash or put directly in dishwasher
  • Clothes: Put in hamper, closet, or wash immediately
  • Keys/wallet: Designated spot by the door

üí° Habit Stacking Tips

Make It Automatic

Attach new organizing habits to things you already do:

  • After I pour my morning coffee, I'll load the dishwasher
  • After I brush my teeth, I'll make my bed
  • After I take off my shoes, I'll put them in their designated spot
  • After I finish dinner, I'll do a 5-minute pickup

üéØ Getting Started

Week 1: Choose ONE Habit

Don't try to implement everything at once. Pick the habit that will make the biggest impact in your home and focus on that for one week.

Popular starter habits:

  • Making bed every morning
  • Washing dishes after each meal
  • 5-minute evening pickup
  • Putting shoes away immediately

Week 2 and beyond: Once your first habit feels automatic (usually 7-10 days), add one more. Build slowly for lasting change.
