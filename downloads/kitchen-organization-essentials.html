Cabinet Optimization

The Triangle Principle

Keep your most-used items within arm's reach of your main work triangle (sink, stove, refrigerator).

  • ‚ñ° Store everyday dishes at eye level
  • ‚ñ° Keep rarely used items on highest shelves
  • ‚ñ° Use shelf risers to double storage space
  • ‚ñ° Install under-cabinet lighting for visibility

  • ‚ñ° Store heavy items in lower cabinets
