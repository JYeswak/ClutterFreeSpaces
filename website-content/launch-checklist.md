## Booking Management

### When You Get an Inquiry:
1. Respond within 2 hours (ideally 30 min)
2. Schedule free consultation call
3. Send calendar invite if they book
4. Follow up 24 hours before appointment

### When Someone Books:
1. Send confirmation text/email immediately
2. Ask for address and any special access notes
3. Request they text you day-of to confirm
4. Add to your calendar with buffer time

### Track Your Spots:
```
Total Available: 8 clients
Booked: ___
In Consultation: ___
Remaining: ___
```

Update social posts when spots fill up to create urgency!
