Maintaining Your Organized Space

Daily Habits (5 minutes)

  • Make beds and tidy bedrooms
  • Clear kitchen counters after use
  • Sort mail immediately
  • Put items back in designated spots
  • One-touch rule for handling items

Weekly Tasks (30 minutes)

  • Declutter one small problem area
  • File accumulated paperwork
  • Reorganize high-traffic zones
  • Plan for upcoming events or seasons
  • Reset family organization systems

Seasonal Reviews (2 hours)

  • Rotate seasonal clothing and gear
  • Evaluate and adjust storage systems
  • Declutter unused or outgrown items
  • Update labels and organization zones
  • Plan for upcoming season's needs
