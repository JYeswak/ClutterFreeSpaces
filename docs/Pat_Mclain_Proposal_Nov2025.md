### APPROACH

**The ClutterFree Spaces Method for Downsizing:**

1. **Assess** - Walk through the entire space to understand the scope and your priorities
2. **Categorize** - Sort items into Keep, Donate, Discard, and "Maybe" piles
3. **Decide** - Work through the "Maybe" pile together with gentle guidance
4. **Remove** - Get donation and discard items OUT of the home promptly
5. **Organize** - Arrange remaining items in functional, accessible systems
6. **Maintain** - Set up simple systems so organization sticks

**My Promise:** This is a judgment-free zone. Downsizing is emotional, and I'm here to support you through every decision at your pace.
