Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?
We serve all of Missoula and surrounding areas within 30 miles. For RV organization, we travel throughout Western Montana including Kalispell, Bozeman, and Helena (travel fees may apply for distances over 30 miles).
Do I need to buy organizing products before you arrive?
No! We'll assess your space first and create a custom plan. We can shop for products together, you can purchase from our list, or we can work with what you have. Many clients are surprised how much we can accomplish with items they already own.
Will you judge me for my mess?
Never! We've seen it all and approach every space with compassion and zero judgment. Our founder Chanel has ADHD and understands that life happens. We're here to help, not judge.
How long does a typical project take?
It varies by space: Kitchen (3-6 hours), Bedroom (3-4 hours), Garage (6-8 hours), RV (6-8 hours), Whole home (20-40 hours). We'll give you a time estimate during your free consultation.
What if I need help maintaining the organization?
We set up systems designed to be maintained easily. Every project includes a custom maintenance plan. We also offer quarterly "tune-up" sessions at a discounted rate for past clients.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes! For projects over $500, we offer payment plans. We accept cash, check, Venmo, and credit cards. A 50% deposit reserves your date.
Can you work with my ADHD brain?
Absolutely! Our founder has ADHD and specializes in creating visual, easy-to-maintain systems that work WITH your brain, not against it. We use clear containers, labels, and logical zones that make sense for how you think.
