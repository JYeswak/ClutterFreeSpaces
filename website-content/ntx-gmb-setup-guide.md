## STEP-BY-STEP SETUP

### **Step 1: Create Google Business Profile**

1. Go to: **https://business.google.com**
2. Click **"Manage now"** or **"Add your business"**
3. Sign in with Google account: `contact@clutter-free-spaces.com` (recommended) OR personal Gmail

**IMPORTANT:** Use the same Google account for Google Search Console, Analytics, etc. for easier management

---

### **Step 2: Enter Business Name**

**Business Name:** `ClutterFreeSpaces - Professional Organizing`

**Why this format:**
- Primary: ClutterFreeSpaces (brand name)
- Descriptor: Professional Organizing (helps with search)
- Clean, professional, matches Montana listing

**DON'T:**
- Add location to name (violates Google guidelines)
- Use keywords spam ("Best Organizer Denton Texas") - can get suspended
- Use all caps or special characters

### **Step 3: Choose Business Category**

**Primary Category:** Professional Organizer

**Search for:** "professional organizer" - it should auto-suggest

### **Step 7: Verification**

Google will ask to verify your business.

**For Service Area Businesses:**

**Option 1: Postcard Verification** (Most Common)
- Google mails postcard to business address
- Takes 5-14 days to arrive
- Contains verification code

**Problem:** You're temporarily in Texas, postcard goes to Montana

**Solution:**
- Have someone in Montana receive and text you the code
- OR use alternative verification if offered

**Option 2: Phone/Email Verification** (Sometimes Offered)
- Instant verification
- Only offered to some businesses
- If available, use this!

**Option 3: Video Verification** (Sometimes Offered)
- Record short video of business (you talking about services)
- Upload to Google
- Reviewed within 2-5 days

**What to do:**
Try to get phone/email verification. If not available, use Montana address for postcard and coordinate with someone there.

## REVIEW GENERATION STRATEGY

### **Why Reviews Matter:**
- Google ranks businesses with more reviews higher
- Reviews build trust (social proof)
- 5-star average = credibility

### **How to Get Reviews:**

**Step 1: Create Review Link**
1. Go to your GMB dashboard
2. Click "Get more reviews"
3. Copy your short review link
4. Example: `g.page/r/[your-code]/review`

**Step 2: Share Link with Clients**

**Text Message (Best Method):**
```
Hi [Client Name]! It was a pleasure organizing your home. If you have 2 minutes, would you mind leaving a quick Google review? It helps other North Texas families find me! [REVIEW LINK] Thanks so much! - Chanel
```

**Email Follow-Up:**
```
Subject: Quick favor?

Hi [Client Name],

Thank you again for trusting ClutterFreeSpaces with your home! I loved creating organized systems for your [space].

If you have 2 minutes, I'd be so grateful for a Google review. It really helps other families in North Texas find my services!

[REVIEW LINK BUTTON]

Thanks so much!
Chanel
```

**Step 3: Timing**
- Send review request IMMEDIATELY after service (same day)
- Follow up 2 days later if no review
- Don't be pushy - one follow-up maximum

### **Responding to Reviews:**

**5-Star Review Response:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! It was a pleasure helping you create an organized [space]. So glad the systems are working for your family! Enjoy your clutter-free home! üòä - Chanel
```

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### **"My business isn't showing up in Google Maps"**
- **Solution:** Can take 1-2 weeks after verification. Be patient.
- Post regularly, add photos, get reviews to speed up indexing

### **"I can't verify my business"**
- **Solution:** Try different verification methods. Contact Google My Business support.

### **"My listing was suspended"**
- **Reason:** Usually guideline violations (keyword stuffing, fake address, etc.)
- **Solution:** Read Google guidelines, fix violations, request reinstatement

### **"Competitors have fake reviews"**
- **Solution:** Focus on getting REAL reviews. Report fake reviews if egregious.
- Don't engage in fake review tactics - Google is getting better at detecting
