Rule #3: Keep It Simple

üè† Room-by-Room Simple Solutions

Kitchen: The 3-Zone System

  • Zone 1: Cook (stove, pots, utensils)
  • Zone 2: Clean (sink, soap, towels)
  • Zone 3: Store (pantry, dishes, food)

Simple Rule: Everything stays in its zone. No exceptions.

Bedroom: The One-Drawer Method

  • Each person gets ONE drawer for everything
  • Clothes that don't fit in the drawer get donated
  • Hanging clothes get ONE rod section each
  • Shoes get ONE designated spot

Bathroom: The Basket System

  • Each person gets ONE basket for all toiletries
  • Community items stay on counter or in medicine cabinet
  • If basket is full, something has to go

‚ö° Daily Maintenance (10 Minutes Max)

Evening Reset Checklist

  • Put dirty dishes in sink or dishwasher
  • Return items to their designated homes
  • Wipe down counters and surfaces
  • Set out tomorrow's clothes
  • Do a 2-minute pickup walkthrough

üì¶ Simple Storage Solutions

The One-Bin Rule

  • Tools: One toolbox with essentials only
  • Cleaning: One caddy with all supplies
  • First Aid: One kit with everything
  • Office: One box with papers and supplies

Emergency Organization

The 5-Minute Rule: If you can't find something in 5 minutes, your system needs work.

  • Keep a "homeless items" basket for quick cleanup
  • Deal with the basket once a week
  • If something is always homeless, find it a permanent spot
