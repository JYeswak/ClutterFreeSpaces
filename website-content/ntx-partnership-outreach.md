### **Opening:**
"Hi [Name]! This is Chanel from ClutterFreeSpaces - I sent you an email about partnering to offer organizing services to your clients. Do you have 5 minutes to chat?"

### **If They Remember Email:**
"Great! So here's what I'm thinking... [explain specific partnership value for their business]"

### **If They Don't Remember:**
"No problem! Quick recap: I'm a professional organizer visiting North Texas through December, and I help families with unpacking, organizing, and decluttering. I thought we might be able to refer clients to each other. Does that sound interesting?"

### **Objection Handling:**

**"We don't usually do referral partnerships"**
"I totally understand. What if we just tried it with one client? No commitment, just see if it adds value for your customers?"

**"We're too busy right now"**
"Perfect! That means you have clients who could use this service. Can I send you my info so you can refer when the timing is right?"

**"How much is the referral commission?"**
"For real estate agents and movers, I offer $50 per booked client. For ongoing partnerships, we can discuss percentage-based commissions. What would feel fair to you?"

### **Closing:**
"Great! Can I send you my one-page partnership info sheet? What's the best email?"
