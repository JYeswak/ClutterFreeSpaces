# North Texas Facebook Groups Strategy
## ClutterFreeSpaces Community Engagement Plan

**Goal:** Build trust, provide value, generate leads through authentic community engagement
**Approach:** Give first, sell second
**Timeline:** 5-week campaign with ongoing presence

## PRIMARY TARGET GROUPS

### **1. Denton County Moms**
- **Platform:** Facebook + Website (dentoncountymoms.com)
- **Handle:** @DentonCoMoms
- **Size:** 15,000+ active members (estimated)
- **Demographics:** Moms with kids, busy families, homeowners
- **Activity Level:** High (multiple posts daily)
- **Moderation:** Active (read rules before posting)

**Why This Group Matters:**
- Direct access to decision-makers (moms organize homes!)
- High engagement on service recommendations
- Word-of-mouth referrals within group

**Engagement Strategy:**
- Like/comment on 3-5 posts daily (be genuinely helpful)

## GROUP ENGAGEMENT RULES

### **THE GOLDEN RULES:**

1. **Give 10x More Than You Take**
   - For every service post, provide 10 helpful comments/tips

2. **Never Spam**
   - Post about services 1-2x per group MAX during 6 weeks
   - Always provide value in post

3. **Follow Group Rules**
   - Read pinned posts/group rules BEFORE posting
   - Some groups have "Service Sunday" or specific days for businesses

4. **Be Authentic**
   - Real photos, real stories, real help
   - No stock images or copy-paste posts

5. **Engage Before You Post**
   - Be active in group for 2-3 days before posting services
   - Build rapport and trust first
